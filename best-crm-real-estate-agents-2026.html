üéØ What Real Estate Agents Need in a CRM (Core Features in 2026)

Before choosing a CRM, understand what matters most for real estate professionals:

1. MLS Integration

Allows you to sync listings, update property data, and share property info directly with clients.

2. Lead Capture & Routing

The CRM should easily connect with:

  • Facebook Ads
  • Zillow, Realtor.com, Trulia
  • Website forms & landing pages
  • Open house apps

And then route leads to the right agent.

3. Automated Follow-Up

Key tools include:

  • SMS automation
  • Drip email campaigns
  • Instant response texts
  • Smart action plans based on lead behavior

4. Pipeline & Deal Tracking

Agents need a visual pipeline for:

New lead ‚Üí Contact made ‚Üí Showing ‚Üí Offer ‚Üí Under contract ‚Üí Closed

5. Communication Tools

A great CRM supports:

  • Calling directly from the CRM
  • Text messaging
  • Email sequences
  • Conversation tracking across channels

6. Property & Transaction Management

Ability to track: Properties, Clients, Showings, Contracts, Tasks

7. Mobile App

Agents must work from: Phone, Tablet, Laptop

8. Marketing Tools

The best CRMs include:

  • Landing pages
  • IDX websites
  • Social media campaign tools
