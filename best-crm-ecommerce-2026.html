Why E-commerce Needs Specialized CRM

E-commerce operates differently from traditional B2B. Your CRM must handle fast-paced, high-volume transactions.

High-frequency purchases

You're tracking thousands of customers, orders, upsells, abandoned carts, and retention cycles.

Behavior-based segmentation

Required segments: First-time buyers, Repeat customers, High LTV customers, Window shoppers, Cart abandoners, Discount-only buyers, VIP customers.

Automated revenue workflows
