8. Best Practices to Maximize ROI

The Essential "Big 4" Flows (You MUST Have These)

  1. Abandoned Cart Flow (3-4 emails over 72 hours)
  2. Welcome Series (3 emails introducing brand + discount)
  3. Post-Purchase (thank you + upsell + review request)
  4. Browse Abandonment (viewed but didn't add to cart)

Additional High-ROI Flows

  • Win-back flow (customers inactive 60-90 days)
  • Replenishment reminders (for consumable products)
  • Cross-sell sequences (based on purchase history)
  • VIP customer flows (high CLV segment)

Segmentation Strategies That Work

  • VIP Customers: Spent $300+ lifetime
  • High CLV: Predicted lifetime value >$500
  • Predicted to Purchase: AI says 70%+ likelihood in next 30 days
  • About to Churn: High churn risk score
  • Repeat Buyers: 2+ purchases
  • Seasonal Buyers: Purchase patterns by season

Other Critical Tips

  • Use SMS strategically: Only for high-value carts, flash sales, and key events
  • A/B test religiously: Subject lines, send times, discounts weekly
  • Add dynamic blocks: Personalized product recommendations in every email
  • Clean your list: Suppress unengaged subscribers every 90 days
  • Track revenue per flow: Know which automations make money
  • Don't over-email: 2-3 campaigns per week max
