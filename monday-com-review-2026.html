    14. FAQ

    Q1: Is Monday.com worth the price for small teams?

    Yes, if those 5-10 people manage more than 3 active projects at once and value visual clarity. The time saved on status meetings typically pays for the subscription within the first month.

    Q2: Can I use Monday.com for free?

    Technically yes, but only for 2 users with severely limited features (no automations, no Gantt charts). For a "business," the answer is effectively no. You need at least the Basic plan ($9/user).

    Q3: How does Monday.com compare to Asana?

    Monday is more "Lego-like" and visual with color-coded boards. Asana is more "List-like" and structured with better task dependencies. See our detailed Asana vs Monday comparison.

    Q4: Does Monday.com have time tracking?

    Yes, but only on the Pro plan ($19/user) and above. The time tracking is built-in but doesn't generate payroll-ready timesheets without third-party integrations.

    Q5: How many users can I have on the free plan?

    Maximum 2 users on the free plan. Paid plans require a minimum of 3 seats, even if you only have 1-2 people using it.

    Q6: Can clients/guests access boards for free?

    Depends on your plan. On Standard, every 4 guests count as 1 paid seat. On Pro and above, guests are unlimited and completely free.

    Q7: Is Monday.com good for Agile/Scrum teams?

    Yes, it has Kanban boards and Sprint planning features, but Jira is still the gold standard for pure software development teams. Monday works well for hybrid teams (dev + marketing + sales).

    Q8: Can I migrate from Trello or Asana to Monday.com?

    Yes. Trello import is built-in and seamless. Asana requires CSV export/import. Most data transfers successfully, but expect to rebuild automations and custom workflows (1-2 weeks).

    Q9: What's the difference between Standard and Pro plans?

    Main differences: Pro adds time tracking, formula columns, dependency columns, unlimited free guests, and 25,000 automation actions/month (vs 250 on Standard). Pro is worth it for teams managing complex projects with client access.

    Q10: Does Monday.com work offline?

    Partially. The mobile app allows offline viewing of boards and tasks. You can make updates offline, which sync when you're back online. The web app requires an internet connection.
