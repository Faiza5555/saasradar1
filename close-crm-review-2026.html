2. What is Close CRM?

Close was founded by Steli Efti and the team behind ElasticSales. Unlike other CRM founders who came from engineering or database backgrounds, the Close team were cold-calling experts. They built the tool they wanted for themselves: one that eliminated the "alt-tab" fatigue of switching between a CRM, a VOIP phone, and an email client.

Core Philosophy: Velocity Above All

The core philosophy of Close is that data entry is the enemy of sales. In 2026, Close remains the only CRM that treats calling, SMS, and email as "first-class citizens" baked directly into the lead view. There are no "plugins" required to make a call.

2026 Positioning

While HubSpot has become a "Customer Platform" and Salesforce an "Enterprise Operating System," Close has doubled down on the Inside Sales niche. It positions itself against "bloated" CRMs by offering 50% faster implementation times.

3. Key Features (2026)

Built-in Power Dialer

This remains the "killer feature." On the Professional plan and up, the Power Dialer allows reps to work through a list of leads automatically. As soon as one call ends, the next begins. It eliminates the "hesitation gap" between cold calls.

AI Features 2026: Conversation Intelligence

  • Call Assistant: Automatically transcribes and summarizes every call ($0.02/min)
  • AI Call Scoring: AI flags "objection moments" or "competitor mentions" for review
  • Email Suggestions: AI suggests phrasing based on sentiment of your last call

Multi-Channel Sequences

Build "Workflows" that combine automated email on Day 1, Power Dialer task on Day 2, and native SMS on Day 3. Because phone and SMS are native, tracking is 100% accurate without "syncing" delays.

Smart Views

Dynamic filters instead of folders. Example: "Leads in California + Not called in 48 hours + Last email opened." As soon as you call them, they disappear from the list automatically.

13. FAQ

Q1: Is Close CRM worth the price for small teams?

Yes, if your team makes 30+ calls per day per rep. The time saved on call logging and app-switching typically pays for itself within the first month. For low-call-volume teams, stick with cheaper options like Pipedrive.

Q2: How does Close compare to Pipedrive for sales teams?

Close wins for high-velocity outbound teams (50+ calls/day) due to built-in calling. Pipedrive wins for visual pipeline management and lower price. See our detailed CRM comparison.

Q3: Does Close work for inbound sales or only outbound?

Close works for both, but shines for outbound. For primarily inbound sales, HubSpot offers better marketing integration and web forms. Close's strength is in proactive calling and follow-up sequences.

Q4: Can I use Close without making calls?

Technically yes, but you'd be overpaying. Close's pricing is justified by the built-in calling infrastructure. If you don't make calls, use Pipedrive ($14/user) or HubSpot Free instead.

Q5: How many calling minutes are included?

Calling is usage-based, not included. US/Canada calls cost ~$0.01/minute. You get $10 in calling credits to start. International rates vary. Budget $30-60/month per active calling rep.

Q6: Is there a free trial?

Yes, Close offers a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start the trial.

Q7: Can I migrate from Salesforce or HubSpot to Close?

Yes, via CSV export/import. Most contact and deal data transfers successfully. Expect to rebuild custom workflows and sequences (1-2 weeks). Close offers migration support on Enterprise plans.

Q8: Is Close good for remote sales teams?

Excellent for remote teams. Cloud-based calling means reps can work from anywhere with internet. Call recording and AI transcription help managers coach remote reps effectively.

Q9: What integrations does Close support?

300+ integrations including Zapier (connects to 3,000+ apps), Slack, Calendly, Zoom, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, and all major email providers (Gmail, Outlook, IMAP).

Q10: Does Close have predictive dialing?

Yes, but only on the Enterprise plan ($139/user/month). Predictive dialing automatically calls multiple numbers simultaneously and connects answered calls to available reps‚Äîideal for teams of 15+ making 100+ calls/day each.
